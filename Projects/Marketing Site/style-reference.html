05 — Buttons

Six pill buttons

All buttons share the same shape: 32px tall, 999px radius, 14/500 type, 18px horizontal padding. Variants change surface, stroke and mode (over light vs. over dark imagery).

btn-primary

Brand purple. Hover deepens to #5b3bb0. Default page CTA.

btn-ghost

Transparent on neutral surfaces with a 1px border. Hover fills with --bg-grey.

btn-soft

Frosted white pill. rgba(255,255,255,0.92) + 8px backdrop blur. Designed to sit over hero imagery.

btn-dark

Charcoal pill — used inside capability and latest cards. Hover deepens to #1a1a1e.

btn-stroke-light

White-stroke pill. Lives over dark imagery (card overlays, research band). Hover frosts a faint white wash with backdrop blur.

btn-ghost (over Hero v2)

Same btn-ghost class — repainted by .hero-variant-2 .site-header .header-actions .btn-ghost when it sits over hero imagery: rgba(255,255,255,0.12) background, 12px backdrop blur, 0.4 white stroke. Same override recolors btn-soft on the v2 hero so they pair.

09 — Border radius

Graduated radius scale

Size-aware: smaller surfaces use a tighter curve so the radius reads as proportionally consistent at every scale. Five values cover the system — four rectangular sizes that step up with surface area, plus 999px for anything pill-shaped.

8px Mega-menu hover background
12px --radius / --radius-media · all media cards (cap, latest, gallery, research thumbs, feature card, research panel)
14px .nav-dropdown card (steps between item pills and hero frame)
16px Hero v2 frame · about-page hero
999px Buttons · v2 logo pill · eyebrow chips
