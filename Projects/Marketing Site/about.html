AI that transforms how brands
become part of the content people love.

View Careers

For decades, advertising and content have existed in opposition — interrupting each other and competing for the same attention. That separation is ending. AI now makes it possible for brands to live inside the stories, shows, and social moments people actually choose to watch — not beside them, not before them, but woven into them.

Rembrand is the platform built for this shift. Our in-content formats embed brands directly into the viewing experience, seen without being intrusive. Our social content ads take what already works — authentic, engaging social experiences — and integrate them seamlessly into the programmatic ad tech stack, extending them across every screen, every network, every moment.

Seen by viewers as content rather than advertising, at the same frequency, cadence, quality and consistency they've come to expect on social — now available everywhere. The result isn't advertising that tries harder. It's advertising that finally meets consumers where they already are.
