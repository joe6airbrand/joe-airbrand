Social Display

Your best posts, sized for every banner.

Drop in a post URL and Rembrand rebuilds it as a programmatic banner that keeps the look, the comments, the likes — the whole social feel. Slots into standard display placements, runs at campaign scale, and skips the production cycle entirely.

  • Zero production cost — a post URL is your asset.
  • Engagement metrics tracked end-to-end.
  • Beats banner blindness with a familiar feed feel.
